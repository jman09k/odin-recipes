copycat sizzler cheese toast

Decription

amazing toast.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons butter, softened
  • 1/4 cup finely grated Romano cheese
  • 1 pinch dried parsley
  • 1 pinch garlic powder
  • 3 slices artisan-style bread

    • Steps

    1. Stir softened butter, Romano cheese, parsley, and garlic powder together in a small bowl. Spread 2 teaspoons mixture on one side of each slice of bread.
    2. Place a heavy skillet over medium high heat, and add each slice of bread butter side down, and cook until golden brown, 45 seconds to 1 minute. Bread will be done very quickly. Check after 45 seconds.
